## A Quarto Manuscript Template

Impacts of different salinity conditions on physiology of hairy shore crabs and European green crabs.
Materials:
- 14 hairy shore crabs (4 per condition + 2 for initial hemolymph measurements)
- 3 EU green crab
- 15 bee tags
- refractometer for hemolymph osmolality
- hemolymph extraction materials needed for 17 extrcations: needles and syringes
- scale for massing
- timer for righting time
- InstantOcean for salinity adjustments
- Seatables and dividers
- enough Resazurin for 45 respiration replicates
